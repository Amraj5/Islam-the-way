Welcome to the Path of Peace

Islam is a religion of peace, mercy, and forgiveness. Learn more below.

The 5 Pillars of Islam

Shahadah

Declaration of Faith: Belief in the oneness of Allah and Muhammad (SAW) as His Messenger.

Salah

Prayer: Performing the five daily prayers.

Zakah

Charity: Giving to those in need and supporting the community.

Sawm

Fasting: Observing fasts during the month of Ramadan.

Hajj

Pilgrimage: Journey to the holy city of Mecca once in a lifetime.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Islam mean?

Islam means "submission" to the will of Allah and promoting peace.

Who is Allah?

Allah is the Arabic word for God, the one and only Creator.
